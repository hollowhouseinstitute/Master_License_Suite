# HHI-LIC-01  
## Master Licensing & Governance Standard for Behavioral AI Materials

### Status
Active — Canonical

### Issuing Authority
Hollow House Institute

---

## 1. Purpose

HHI-LIC-01 defines the minimum licensing, access, and governance requirements for behavioral, relational, and longitudinal AI materials produced by Hollow House Institute.

This standard exists to:
- Prevent misuse of sensitive behavioral data
- Prohibit unauthorized AI system training
- Enforce ethical and governance boundaries
- Maintain longitudinal integrity of behavioral datasets

---

## 2. Applicability

This standard applies to all:
- Datasets
- Schemas
- Metadata
- Codex materials
- Symbolic systems
- Governance frameworks
- Audit tools
- Derived or transformed artifacts

---

## 3. Core Invariants (Non-Bypassable)

Any compliant use MUST satisfy:

1. Explicit written authorization
2. Attribution to Hollow House Institute
3. No AI training or parameter influence
4. No derivative exploitation
5. No commercial or indirect commercial benefit
6. No behavioral manipulation or identity harm

Violation of any invariant constitutes non-compliance.

---

## 4. License Hierarchy

1. HHI-LIC-01 (this standard)
2. Master License Suite repository
3. Individual license instruments
4. Repository-specific documentation

Lower-order documents may not override higher-order constraints.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Compliance is enforced through:
- Access revocation
- License termination
- Legal remedies
- Governance escalation

---

## 6. Auditability

A system is compliant only if:
- Use can be independently verified
- Training exclusion is provable
- Derivative absence is demonstrable
- Attribution is visible and persistent

---

## 7. Relationship to AI Governance

HHI-LIC-01 is designed to interoperate with:
- Longitudinal Behavioral Governance for AI Systems
- Institutional audit frameworks
- Regulatory review processes
